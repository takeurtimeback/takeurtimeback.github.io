About

TAKE YOUR TIME - A NO-WORKING SPACE is a community building project that grows around art like certain plants might grow on a fence.

TAKE YOUR TIME is not what we do, it's how we do things - it is the values and habits we accept and foster:

Mutual Support

Shared Resources

Community Space

Radical Education

OUR EVENTS

Currently, our community's main offering is a steady stream of DIY shows featuring a huge variety of underground artists, performers and other such freaks.

OUR COMMUNITY

But beyond that, it is one of our main priorities that community members feel the freedom and opportunity to make offerings of their own so far these have included art markets, workshops, movie screenings, meditation groups, music recording, and other such nonsense.
